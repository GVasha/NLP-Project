Foreigner Procedures
Procedures and Procedures
Extension of short-term stay
To extend, exceptionally, the stay of foreign persons who have entered Spain for purposes other than their residence, work or long term stay.
Requirements and conditions (https://sede.policia.gob.es/portalCiudadano/_ca-valencia/tramites_extranjeria_tramite_asignacion_nie.php)
Those who are in Spain in one of the following cases will be in a situation of short stay:
The person who holds a short-stay visa, during its period of validity.
Anyone who is in Spain for an expected stay of a duration not exceeding ninety calendar days within any period of one hundred and eighty calendar days and is not subject to the visa requirement as established by European Union law.
Who, exceptionally, has been authorized to stay in Spanish territory for a duration not exceeding ninety calendar days within any period of one hundred and eighty calendar days under the terms of article 51 of RD 1155/2024, of November 19.
Classes and typologies
Extension of stay without a visa.
Extension of the short-stay visa.
Who are the stakeholders?
Foreign persons who have entered Spain for purposes other than their residence, work or long-term stay.
Duration of the extension
In the case of
entry with visa
, when the duration of the visa is less than three months, the stay may be extended, which in no case may be more than three months in a period of six.
In the event of
entry without a visa
, when exceptional circumstances so warrant, the stay of a foreigner foreigner in Spanish territory may be authorized beyond three months.

Ordinary passport or travel document, valid longer than the length of stay requested, which will be recorded in the file and returned to the interested party.
Accreditation of the reasons given for the application, which must be exceptional, in the case of nationals of States that do not require a visa for entry into Spain and of a humanitarian nature, force majeure or serious personal reasons in the case of nationals of States that do require a visa for entry into Spain.
Sufficient proof that it has adequate financial means for the extension time requested, in the terms established in title I, Regime of entry and exit from Spanish territory, of RD 1155/2024, of 19 November.
Travel assistance insurance with the same coverage as that required for the application of the short-stay visa, and with a validity equal to or greater than the extension requested.
Guarantees of return to the country of origin or, where appropriate, of admission to the third State of destination, prior to the date of completion of the extension requested. The contribution of a ticket purchased in the name of the applicant with a closed date of return prior to the end of the period of extension of stay requested may serve as evidence of this circumstance.

Resolution of the procedure:
Bodies competent to resolve this procedure:
The extension of stay may be granted by the Sub-Delegates of the Government, by the Government Delegates in the Uniprovincial Autonomous Communities, and by the General Commissioner for Foreigners and Borders of the General Directorate of the Police, after a report from the Superior Headquarters or Police Station.
The resolution of the procedure does put an end to the administrative route.
Effect of the lack of resolution in this period: Dismissal (initiation at the request of the interested party).
Regulations governing administrative silence:
