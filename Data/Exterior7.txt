Procedures and Procedures
Registration certificate
It is a printed document that is provided to the foreigner that cannot be documented by the authorities of any country and that wishes to be documented by Spain, once its registration in the Central Register of Foreigners has been resolved.
Printed document, to be renewed annually and the characteristics of which will be determined by the Ministry of the Interior, following a report by the Inter-Ministerial Commission on Foreigners
Requirements and conditions (https://sede.policia.gob.es/portalCiudadano/_ca-valencia/tramites_extranjeria_tramite_asignacion_nie.php)
Who are the stakeholders?
Foreigner who cannot be documented by the authorities of any country and who wishes to be documented by Spain.
Period of validity
1 year, renewable.

Basic data of the procedure:
Resolution of the procedure:
Bodies competent to resolve this procedure:
Delegate or Subdelegate of the Government or the Commissioner General for Foreigner and Borders
Maximum period for resolving and notifying: 3 months.
The resolution of the procedure does put an end to the administrative route.
Effect of the lack of resolution in this period: Dismissal (initiation at the request of the interested party).
Regulations governing administrative silence:
Royal Decree 1155/2024, of 19 November, approving the Regulation of Organic Law 4/2000, of 11 January, on the rights and freedoms of foreigners in Spain and their social integration.
Resources that can be interposed:
Replacement
Organ that resolves the resource: Organ that dictated the act.
Period for lodging an appeal: 1 month.
Contentious - Administrative
Organ that resolves the resource:
Contentious Court - Administrative.
Central Administrative Litigation Courts; Administrative Litigation Chambers of the High Court of Justice.
Administrative Litigation Division of the National High Court; Administrative Litigation Division of the Supreme Court.
Period for lodging an appeal: 2 months.
