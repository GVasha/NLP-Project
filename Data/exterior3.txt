Assignment of NIE at the request of the interested party

Assignment of NIE for foreigners who have relations with Spain due to economic, professional, or social interests.

Foreigners who have relations with Spain due to economic, professional, or social interests must request the assignment of the Foreigners Identification Number (NIE) from the Directorate General of the Police.

Requirements and Conditions

(https://sede.policia.gob.es/portalCiudadano/_ca-valencia/tramites_extranjeria_tramite_asignacion_nie.php
)

Not being in Spain in an irregular situation.

The reasons for requesting the assignment of this number must be communicated.

The procedure must be resolved within a maximum period of five days from the entry of the application in the register of the competent body responsible for processing it. The decision must be notified within this period. If no response is received within this time, the application will be considered rejected.

Basic procedure data:

Procedure resolution:

Competent authority to resolve this procedure:
By own competence:
Directorate General of the Police

Maximum period to resolve and notify:
The procedure must be resolved within a maximum period of five days from the entry of the application in the register of the competent body.

The resolution of the procedure ends the administrative route.

Effect of lack of resolution within this period:
Rejection (initiated at the request of the interested party).

Appeals that may be filed:

Reconsideration (Reposición)
Authority that resolves the appeal: The body that issued the act.
Deadline to file the appeal: 1 month.

Contentious-Administrative Appeal
Authority that resolves the appeal:
Administrative Litigation Court.
Central Administrative Litigation Courts; Administrative Litigation Chambers of the High Court of Justice.
Administrative Litigation Chamber of the National Court; Administrative Litigation Chamber of the Supreme Court.
Deadline to file the appeal: 2 months.
